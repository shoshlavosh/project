Welcome to Tenant Helper!

When you're looking for a place to live, it's hard to know what's going on "under the hood" of a particular apartment. This website sources historical data about complaints and violations from the San Francisco Department of Building Inspection, and displays reviews from tenants who have shared their experience of what it was like to live at an address, as a renter.
To view building data, leave a review, or read other reviews, you'll first need to go to the page of a specific address. You can either do that by searching for a particular address, or by choosing a building from a list by of buildings by address.
